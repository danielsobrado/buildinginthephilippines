## Residential Construction in Nueva Ecija - Project Bill of Materials by Randy Yerro

I've found [this video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fe2-z-yRkME&feature=youtu.be) by [Randy Yerro](https://www.facebook.com/dhoy.ranz.9/) and I decided to have a look at the costs as a reference.

Location: Nueva Ecija, Philippines
Total Project Cost: PHP 1,520,633 (USD 30,412)
Building Area: 150 sqm (15m x 10m)
Lot Area: 430 sqm

## Project Overview
- Construction Start: January 18
- Building Features:
  - 3 Bedrooms
  - 2 Toilet and Bath
  - 1 Laundry Room
  - Living Room
  - Dining Area
  - Kitchen
  - Garage
